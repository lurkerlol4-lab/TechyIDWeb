Company Description

Founded in the Philippines, Techy-ID Co. is a forward-thinking technology company dedicated to redefining identity verification in the digital age. By combining scanning technology with simple user interfaces, we provide solutions that allow schools, organizations, and enterprises to verify individuals securely, quickly, and reliably.

About Us

Techy-ID Co. is more than a technology startup — it’s a mission to build trust and efficiency in digital verification systems. Our team is composed of innovative thinkers and engineers driven by the goal of making ID validation more secure and accessible for everyone.

At Techy-ID, we prioritize data security, user privacy, and simplicity. Our core product, IDReaderXP, serves as a modern solution that empowers institutions to authenticate individuals without requiring QR codes or manual checking — ensuring convenience without compromising security.

We continue to innovate through our research in AI-based scanning and real-time ID validation, aiming to become a leader in identity technology solutions across Asia. Techy-ID is proudly built in the Philippines, inspired by local ingenuity and global standards.

Mission: To provide a secure, fast, and accessible platform for digital identity verification that benefits communities, schools, and businesses.

Vision: To become the most trusted and advanced digital ID verification company in Southeast Asia by 2030.
